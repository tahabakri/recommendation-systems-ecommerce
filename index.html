About this demo

A live simulation of how an e-commerce recommender re-ranks the storefront under three engine states. Built for a Machine Learning course — product data is illustrative.

Algorithms after Aggarwal (2016); item-to-item collaborative filtering after Linden et al. (2003); matrix factorisation after Koren et al. (2009).

Recommendation Systems in E-commerce

An interactive teaching demo of how online stores decide what to recommend — cold start, content-based, and collaborative filtering.

It's a simulation, not a trained model: the console traces are scripted to show how each algorithm reasons. No real ML or user data.
