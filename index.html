About this demo

A live simulation of how an e-commerce recommender re-ranks the storefront under three engine states. Built for an academic presentation — product data is illustrative.

Algorithms after Aggarwal (2016); item-to-item collaborative filtering after Linden et al. (2003); matrix factorisation after Koren et al. (2009).
